HKUST Future-Ready Scholars

Introduction to Game Programming using Python
Download files below!

HKUST Future-Ready Scholars was an event held on both 27 April and 4 May 2024 at HKUST. This event was held to give secondary students a chance to prepare for future education while giving students who were enrolled in IDPO 2910 - Undergraduate Teaching Opportunities a chance to try out real-life teaching. A similar page was prepared for students to download lesson materials for the theme above.
You can access the teacher's page here. Or you can return to menu here.
